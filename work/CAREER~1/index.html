Career Mentor System
One Booking, Zero Manual Steps

01

The Challenge

A solo career mentor launching her first paid consultation service — no booking flow, no follow-up process, and no budget for paid software. The entire system had to run from her phone, with no technical background required.

02

What I Built

One connected client journey, automated end to end on free tools:

  • Booking flow: a client books a slot and a branded payment-link email sends itself within minutes — one tap from her phone confirms payment, then the meeting link and intake form follow automatically
  • Post-session: three automated branded emails — thank-you, testimonial request, and follow-up offer
  • A two-database Notion CRM that tracks every client and session at a glance
03

The Outcome

A self-running client journey on 100% free tools — up to five branded emails per client, none written manually. Handed over with full SOPs and troubleshooting docs, tested end-to-end.

Skills: System Design  ·  Apps Script  ·  Calendly  ·  Tally  ·  Notion CRM  ·  Email Automation  ·  Documentation  ·  Handover
